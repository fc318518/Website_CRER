Year: Unknown - The Spark

The Birth of ODDE

In an era long forgotten, a singular mind conceived the foundations of the Organization of Discovery and Development of Existence — ODDE. Though the founder remains unnamed, fragments of early records suggest that a forgotten incident triggered a universal awakening...

Year 2031 - First Resonance

The Echo Frequency

Scientists within the CRER Labs accidentally uncovered an oscillating frequency — one that didn’t behave like anything known to physics. It appeared to transmit data, but encoded in emotion and dreams. Dubbed "The Echo", this resonance would soon change everything.

Year 2044 - Forbidden Vaults

Black Access

After the Mystery Box Project reached Phase 6, an unknown user breached what was referred to as "Black Access" — a vault sealed by non-human protocol. What was found inside remains redacted, but it altered the chain of command within CRER permanently.
