CRER Website Policy

1. Introduction

CRER (Creative Realm of Exploration & Research) is committed to maintaining the privacy, safety, and trust of all users who interact with our platform. This document outlines our key policies regarding content, data collection, user responsibilities, and more.

2. Privacy Policy

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless voluntarily submitted by the user. Any data collected (e.g. for analytics or feedback) will be used solely to improve the platform and will not be sold or shared with third parties.

3. Content Policy

All content shared on CRER must adhere to principles of respect, originality, and integrity. Plagiarism, hate speech, and illegal material are strictly prohibited. Content posted by users reflects their own views, not necessarily those of CRER.

4. Security Policy

We prioritize the digital security of our users. CRER implements reasonable technical safeguards to protect against data breaches and malicious activities. However, no system is 100% secure — users are advised to act with discretion when sharing sensitive information.

5. Intellectual Property

All designs, concepts, names, and custom systems unique to CRER are protected intellectual property unless otherwise stated. Users must credit creators when reusing or referencing any materials from the platform.

6. User Conduct

Users agree not to misuse the site or its tools. Activities such as spamming, unauthorized access attempts, and fraudulent behavior may result in permanent bans and reporting to relevant authorities.

7. Amendments

CRER reserves the right to update this policy at any time. Any significant changes will be communicated through official channels. Continued use of the site implies acceptance of the most recent version of the policy.
